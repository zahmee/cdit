Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

We are Creative Development & IT Programming Est. (CDIT), a registered Saudi business (CR 5855353571) based in Khamis Mushait. This page explains transparently what data we collect through cdit.co and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • Data you submit yourself: when you fill the contact or trial forms or chat with our AI assistant, we may receive your name, phone number, email, business type, and message text.
  • Technical visit data: device and browser type, the page visited, referrer, browser language, and approximate country/city — for statistics and site improvement, without identifying you personally.

Cookies and local storage

  • We use Google measurement tags to measure our advertising campaigns (Google Ads conversions); your browser may store the ad click identifier locally for about 30 days.
  • Your AI assistant chat history is stored in your own browser only (local storage) and is deleted automatically within 24 hours.
  • Small convenience data (such as news caching) stays in your browser and never leaves your device.

How we use your data

  • Contacting you about your request and activating your free trial.
  • Measuring site and campaign performance and improving our services.

We do not sell your data or share it with any third party for marketing purposes.

Technical processors that operate the site

  • Cloudflare: running the site's technical services.
  • Google: advertising conversion measurement.
  • Telegram: delivering contact and trial requests to our team internally.
  • DeepSeek: processing AI assistant chat messages to generate replies — please never type sensitive data (passwords, card numbers, ID numbers) in the chat; the assistant will never ask for them.

Retention and your rights

We keep contact data for as long as needed to follow up on your request and provide the service. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing info@cdit.co.

Regulatory commitment

We handle personal data in line with the requirements of the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law and related regulations.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time; the date above reflects the latest revision.
